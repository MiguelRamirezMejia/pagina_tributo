Nivels of Consciousness

The 8 nivels of Consciousness


  • 1) Beige: Instinctive Awareness. Do what it takes to stay alive.
  • 2) Purple: Tribal Awareness. Seek safety in the group.
  • 3) Red: Power Consciousness. He seeks power over others, he is self-centered.
  • 4) Blue: Normative Awareness. Look for order and be certain in every step.
  • 5) Orange: Practical Awareness. You are always on the move, analyzing and envisioning strategies to prosper.
  • 6) Green: Ecological Consciousness. He is egalitarian and builds relationships with others.
  • 7) Yellow: Integrative Consciousness. He cares about the systems that guarantee success for himself and others.
  • 8) Turquoise:Holistic Awareness. Connected to restoring global harmony.
